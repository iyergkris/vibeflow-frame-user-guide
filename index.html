Accessing the Dashboard

Open a web browser and navigate to http://artframeone.local

Dashboard Features

  • Timer: Shows when the next artwork will be generated.
  • Battery: Displays current battery level and charging status.
  • Generate Now: Instantly creates a new image based on your settings.
  • Upload: Send your own photos to be displayed on the frame.

Customizing Art

By default, the frame picks random art styles. To customize:

  • Custom Prompt: Type a subject (e.g., "A futuristic city") and click Generate Now.
  • Save Prompt: Click Save Prompt Only to keep that subject for future automatic updates.

Uploading Your Own Photos
